DRA. MERYLIN SALVADOR

Dra. Merylin Salvador studied Oral Rehabilitation and Dental Aesthetics in Barcelona. He has worked in clinical techniques in Barcelona and Bilbao, Spain. She is a Specialist in Oral and Maxillofacial Radiology, Diploma in Orthodontics and Orthopedics and Diploma in Endodontics. He is certified in the Invisalign System. He has been performing complete restorations on implants for more than 15 years. He is constantly trained in the latest advances in dental technology and new materials both in Peru and abroad. He has followed CEREC CAD-CAM system courses in Peru and in the USA.
